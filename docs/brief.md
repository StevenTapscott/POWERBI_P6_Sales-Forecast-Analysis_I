# 6. Predictive Sales Forecasting

## Difficulty
Intermediate

---

## Overview

Sales managers need to know where revenue is heading, not just where it's been.

This project gives you hands-on experience with the kind of forward-looking analysis that makes a BI analyst genuinely useful to a business team.

Using historical sales data, you'll build a data model, establish table relationships, and write DAX measures for KPIs and time intelligence functions.

Then you'll implement Power BI's built-in time series forecasting to project future sales trends and visualize confidence intervals.

The final dashboard gives a sales team a single place to track performance against targets and see where the trend line is heading.

---

## What you'll build

A sales performance dashboard with trend lines, DAX-powered KPI cards, and a forecasting visual with confidence bands.
